Toni Aneile’s authentic Italian restaurant concept focuses on "cucina povera"—simple, seasonal, and traditional recipes—inspired by his deep roots in Italian cooking and verified expertise. The experience prioritizes fresh, high-quality ingredients, handcrafted pasta, and a welcoming atmosphere, highlighting regional specialties over Americanized versions.
